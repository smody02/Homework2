How We Started
S&S Eatery started as a product of COMP 20. Two students, Seixas and Sonia had to complete a project where we made a website for a small business: so, S&S Eatery was born! We are proud to bring you the very best in HTML and Hot Coffee. We hope you enjoy our aesthetic and think the food looks yummy. All the photos can be found at @Bisou.Bisou on Instagram.
Our Values
Here at S&S Eatery, we believe in the value of having the highest quality food and websites. All of our coffee and code is made in-house! We value giving back to the community so we donate 5% of our profits to local homeless shelters. If you have any suggestions or would like to learn more, please don't hesitate to contact us!
Restaurant Hours
We are open daily from 7:00am - 4:00pm! Please join us for a meal. Due to COVID-19, all patrons are required to weak a mask. We thank you for adherence to this policy!
